School of Computer Studies

In order to meet the new challenges of the 21st century Information Technology, as well as to achieve the ambition that the industrialization be promoted by modern and sophisticated devices, Arellano University is committed to train talents of new generation on computing and Information Technology, who will have the correct belief in life and the spirit of patriotism and dedication, adhere to the school's motto of judiciousness and fulfillment, have a strong scientific and technological exploration and innovation desire and inherit the good culture of unity, diligence and innovativeness. Our school upholds the high moral quality, strong professional basis, good capability and swift adaptation.


College of Information Technology

ITE offers four-year programs that include the study of computing concepts and theories, algorithmic foundations, new developments in computing, utilization of both hardware and software technologies and management of information systems to provide computing solutions to various users and organizations.
